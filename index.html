I am Gareth Senior

Northerner, Pythonista, Dog Walker, Gentleman, Runner, Optimist & Aspiring Good Egg

About Me

I was born in the Yorkshire town of Holmfirth, a place most famous for BBC sitcom 'The Last Of The Summer Wine' - I am adept at riding down a hill in a bathtub.

I attended Holmfirth High School from 1985-90 where I was unremarkable. I sat A-Levels at Greenhead College in Huddersfield before studying at Edge Hill College, Ormskirk 1992-1995. After a brief career as a journalist & writer I attained a Masters degree from the University of Huddersfield in 1999.

I've lived in North London since February 2000, during that time I've worked as a Designer, Web Developer & Software Engineer for Capital Radio Group, GCap Media plc, BBC Worldwide, EMAP Automotive, Global Radio and Sainsbury's.
